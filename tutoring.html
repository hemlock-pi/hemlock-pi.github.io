What I Offer

I provide one-on-one tutoring in math, computer science, and SAT/ACT prep for students from middle school through undergraduate level. Sessions are available both online and in-person.

Personalized Approach

I custom-make worksheets and digital learning tools for each student every week, tailored to their specific needs and learning style.

Flexible Scheduling

Most students work with me for 1 hour per week, but I'm flexible with scheduling to accommodate your needs.

Pricing

My price is whatever the family wants - I am happy to tutor for free.

Regardless of whatever weekly price we decide on, the first session is always free.
