My Teaching Approach

Personalized Learning

I tailor each session to your specific learning style and goals. Whether you're struggling with a particular concept or want to dive deeper into advanced topics, I adapt my teaching approach accordingly.

My experience as a peer mentor for CS 212 (Mathematical Foundations of Computer Science) has taught me how to break down complex concepts into digestible pieces.

Practical Applications

I believe in connecting theoretical concepts to real-world applications. We'll work through practical examples and projects that help solidify your understanding.

From implementing algorithms to building machine learning models, you'll gain hands-on experience that goes beyond textbook learning.
